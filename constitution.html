League Constitution

Objective

The object of the league is to draft and manage a team of National Football League players to score more points than your opponent in a head-to-head match-up on a weekly basis and win the championship.

League Organization

The league consists of ten (10) teams in a single division.

League Fees

League fees are $50. Dues will be collected at the draft

Prize Money

  • First Place: $300
  • Second Place: $150
  • Third Place: $50

Roster Construction

The maximum roster size at the time of the draft is 25 players: including 9 starters and 16 bench players. 3 IR slots will be available for use after the draft, once waivers clear.

Starting Lineups

  • 1 Quarterback
  • 2 Running Backs
  • 3 Wide Receivers
  • 1 Tight End
  • 1 Flex (RB/WR/TE)
  • 1 Defense/Special Teams

*There is no requirement to fill your lineup each week. Tanking is permitted. Manage your team however you want.

Waivers & Free Agency

FAAB system with a budget of $100 per team. Waiver period is two days.

Trades

Owners may trade players freely, with no trade deadline during the regular season. Trades are approved by the Commissioner. Players acquired via trade during weeks 16-17 may not be started by teams still in contention (see active poll).

Trades may be made during the offseason by contacting the Commissioner and will be tracked on the league website.

Draft Rules

The league draft follows a snake format. Each team retains 17 players, with a five-round draft for new rookies and free agents.

Scoring System

See Yahoo for full scoring settings. Standard 0.5 PPR scoring / 4 point passing TDs.

Schedule

The regular season consists of the first 15 weeks of the NFL season. Playoffs are weeks 16 and 17
