Frequently Asked Questions

When are league dues due?

League fees ($50) must be paid before the draft on August 31st. Failure to pay may result in removal from the league.

How is the draft order determined?

Draft order is snake format and is determined based on the previous season's standings, with the worst team picking first.

When is the roster cut deadline?

Final roster cuts must be submitted in Yahoo by August 23rd.

How are trades processed?

Trades are processed through the league software and are approved by the commissioner (David).
